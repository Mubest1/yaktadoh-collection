Our Story

The Art of Yaktadoh

Yaktadoh Black Label was born from a singular belief: that clothing is more than fabric — it is identity, power, and presence. Located in the heart of Bariga, Lagos, we have been dressing discerning Nigerians with garments that speak before words are said.

Every piece that leaves our studio carries the mark of mastery — hand-measured, hand-cut, and finished with obsessive attention to detail. Because when you wear YBL, you wear Beyond Prestige.
